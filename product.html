THERE'S POWER BELOW THE BOOT

Introducing the POWERFLY Holder – a true, first-of-its-kind holder designed to adapt and respond to every skating motion. Our R&D team took a good hard look at the game's current go-to holder, the Tuuk Lightspeed Edge, and found an opportunity to improve the overall performance output so today’s athletes can maximize each and every shift. The stiff front post increases energy transfer in every stride while the flexible back post delivers faster side-to-side motions to help make you unstoppable and uncatchable on the ice.
